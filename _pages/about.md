I am Kevin Valakuzhy, a PhD candidate at the Georgia Institute of Technology advised by [Fabian Monrose](https://fabianmonrose.github.io/).Before graduate school, I worked as a software engineer at Google and Zeropoint Dynamics. My research focuses on building systems that make binary code analysis more scalable and reliable. I am particularly interested in developing techniques that improve code understanding for both human analysts and automated systems.
