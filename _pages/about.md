I am Kevin Valakuzhy, a PhD candidate at the Georgia Institute of Technology advised by [Fabian Monrose](https://fabianmonrose.github.io/).
Before graduate school, I worked as a software engineer at Google and Zeropoint Dynamics.
My research focuses on building better systems to manage the complexities of binary code analysis.
My interests lie in developing systems to improve understanding of code, for both humans and machines.
